About the Home

Nestled in the embrace of the Aravalli hills, Shreevats is more than a villa — it is a sanctuary designed for those who seek stillness alongside luxury. The property breathes with spiritual intention: an on-site Moksha temple for morning prayers, the Amrit Kund pool reflecting the pale dawn sky, and lawns that stretch toward ancient ridgelines.

With three thoughtfully appointed bedrooms, this retreat welcomes families and small groups seeking an immersive Udaipur experience. The caretaker Laxman ji oversees every detail with warmth and professionalism.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I book only one room?
+
No — the entire home must be booked as a unit. This ensures privacy and exclusivity for your group.
Is smoking or alcohol permitted?
+
Smoking is allowed strictly outdoors in the garden area. Alcohol is permitted within reasonable limits — we discourage late-night revelry or loud music.
Who manages the villa?
+
A dedicated caretaker (Laxman ji) is present on-site and ensures your stay is seamless from arrival to departure.
What is the cancellation policy?
+
75% refund for cancellations 15+ days before check-in. 50% refund for 7–15 days prior. No refund within 7 days of check-in.
What type of beds are available?
+
Bedrooms 1 and 3 feature king-sized beds. Bedroom 2 has a single bed that can be supplemented with a rollaway for an additional guest.
